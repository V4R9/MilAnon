# Grundsätze des Feuerkampfes / Principes du combat par le feu / Principi del combattimento a fuoco

![Illustration showing an observer positioned on high ground with a wide field of vision over a valley with buildings and roads.](image)
**1. Beobachter/Sensor sieht weiter**
Donner à l’observateur/senseur un meilleur champ de vision
L’osservatore/sensore vedono più lontano

![Illustration showing a tactical view where visibility is limited to the engagement range of the weapons, with distance markers "bis 3000 Meter", "200 - 300 Meter", and "400 - 600 Meter".](image)
**2. Nicht weiter sehen, als man treffen will**
Ne pas voir plus loin que son objectif
Non vedere oltre il punto che si vuole colpire

![Illustration showing the use of a river (Natürliche Hindernisse) and a road block (Künstliche Hindernisse) to channel enemy movement.](image)
**3. Natürliche und künstliche Hindernisse ausnützen**
Profiter des obstacles naturels et artificiels
Sfruttare gli ostacoli naturali ed artificiali

![Illustration showing weapons systems positioned to engage targets at specific ranges, labeled "200 - 300 Meter" and "400 - 600 Meter".](image)
**4. Kampfreichweite ausnützen**
Profiter de la portée des armes engagées
Sfruttare la gittata pratica di combattimento

![Illustration showing armored vehicles positioned behind buildings for protection while maintaining a line of fire.](image)
**5. Verteidigungsstarke Stellungen beziehen**
Occuper des positions fortes pour la défense
Occupare posizioni che consentano una buona difesa

![Illustration showing a hidden unit preparing to open fire on an approaching enemy tank from a concealed position.](image)
**6. Überraschende Feuereröffnung sicherstellen**
S’assurer de l’effet de surprise par l’ouverture du feu
Garantire un’apertura del fuoco che sorprenda l’avversario

![Illustration showing multiple units positioned to engage an enemy column from the side (flank).](image)
**7. Flankierenden Einsatz anstreben**
Privilégier les engagements par les flancs
Mirare a un impiego sui fianchi

![Illustration showing multiple fire units concentrating their fire on a single target area.](image)
**8. Feuerkonzentration anstreben**
Privilégier la concentration du feu
Mirare a una concentrazione del fuoco

![Illustration showing primary positions and planned movement paths to alternate positions (Wechselstellungen).](image)
**9. Wechselstellungen vorsehen**
Prévoir des positions de rechange
Prevedere posizioni di ricambio
