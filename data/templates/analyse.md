# Template: Dokumentanalyse

Analyze the attached document thoroughly and provide:

1. **Key Decisions and Orders**
   - What has been decided?
   - Who is responsible for what?
   - What are the deadlines?

2. **Timeline of Events**
   - Chronological list of all dated events, deadlines, and milestones
   - Flag any date conflicts or overlapping events

3. **Open Items / Pending Actions**
   - What requires follow-up?
   - What information is missing?
   - What decisions are still pending?

4. **Risks and Dependencies**
   - What could go wrong?
   - What depends on external factors?
   - Are there resource conflicts?

5. **Questions for Clarification**
   - What is ambiguous or contradictory in the document?
   - What needs to be clarified before execution?
